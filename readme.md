## How to download
To download, go to the [Actions tab](https://github.com/snoopythe3-from-scratch/TrollCube/actions) at the top of the screen. Now find your device on the side of the screen. Click it then you will see a list of actions runs. Go to the first one. Scroll down then click the version you want.
